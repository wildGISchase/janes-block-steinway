Jane's Block!

The “Sidewalk Ballet” along Astoria’s Steinway Street

Thank you for your interest in Jane's Block! We are hard at work building our virtual walk. Please feel free to bookmark this page for now and we look forward to sharing our walk with you right at the start of Jane's Walk weekend on Friday, May 3rd!

For a preview of the type of experience that we are developing, we invite you to check out Jane's Squawk: An Exploration of the Avian World of Queens, our virtual tour from last year's Jane Walk festival.

This site is a collaboration by Stephen Albonesi & Rawnak Zaman. We are researchers based in New York City with an interest in exploring urban life through data visualizations and storytelling.
